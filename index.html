FOUR SEASONS

Memory Game

There are 20 cards with 10 pairs of unique pictures.

The game starts with all the cards face down.

With each move, you turn over two cards of your choice.

If they have the same picture, it's a match and they stay open. Otherwise, the cards are turned face down again.

The game ends when all the cards have been matched.
